WebAbout

The "Vans x Disney The Lion King" collection was released as a collaboration between Vans and Disney, inspired by the beloved animated film. The collection features various shoe designs and apparel items adorned with iconic characters and motifs from The Lion King. The origin of the shoes lies in this collaboration, which brought together the creative styles of Vans and the timeless characters from The Lion King. The collection typically includes sneakers, slip-ons, and apparel such as t-shirts, hoodies, and backpacks featuring characters like Simba, Timon, Pumbaa, Scar, and others.
